# Digital Future in the Rearview Mirror — Andrey Mir

**Format:** Book
**Year:** 2024
**Author:** Andrey Mir (media ecologist; wrote *Postjournalism and the Death of Newspapers*)

## Core Thesis

The digital age is the third great Axial shift in human cognition — following the move from oral to written culture, and from written to print. Mir synthesizes Karl Jaspers' Axial Age theory with Robert Logan's *Alphabet Effect* to argue that the medium we communicate through reshapes thought itself, and digital media is in the middle of rewiring consciousness at a scale we can only see in the rearview mirror.
