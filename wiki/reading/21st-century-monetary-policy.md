# 21st Century Monetary Policy — Ben Bernanke

**Format:** Book
**Year:** 2022
**Subtitle:** *The Federal Reserve from the Great Inflation to COVID-19*
**Author:** Ben Bernanke (former Chair of the Federal Reserve, 2006-2014)

## Core Thesis

A decade-by-decade history of the Fed from the 1970s stagflation era through quantitative easing, the Great Financial Crisis, and COVID-era policy. Bernanke argues that modern monetary policy has had to evolve — new tools, new communication strategies, new frameworks — precisely because the economy has changed structurally: globalization, financialization, a flatter Phillips curve, and the zero lower bound. The old playbook stopped working; this is the new one, written by someone who helped write it.
