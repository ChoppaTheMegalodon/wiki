# Book of Longing — Leonard Cohen

**Format:** Poetry collection (illustrated with Cohen's own drawings)
**Year:** 2006
**Author:** Leonard Cohen

## Core Thesis

A late-career collection of poems, prayers, and sketches, much of it written during Cohen's years at the Mount Baldy Zen Center. Not a thesis — a mood. Longing as a permanent condition. Desire, aging, religious ambiguity, humor, and grief all in the same register, often in the same poem.
