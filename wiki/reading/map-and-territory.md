# The Map and the Territory — Alan Greenspan

**Format:** Book
**Year:** 2013
**Subtitle:** *Risk, Human Nature, and the Future of Forecasting*
**Author:** Alan Greenspan (former Chair of the Federal Reserve, 1987-2006)

## Core Thesis

A post-2008 reflection from the man whose models failed most visibly. Greenspan argues that classical economic models underweight behavioral dynamics — "animal spirits," herd behavior, irrational risk-taking — and the crisis proved that no economic framework survives contact with human nature unless it incorporates them. The map has never been the territory; 2008 was an unusually expensive reminder.
