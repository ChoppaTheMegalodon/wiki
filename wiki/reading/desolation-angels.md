# Desolation Angels — Jack Kerouac

**Format:** Autobiographical novel
**Year:** 1965 (written 1956-1961)
**Author:** Jack Kerouac

## Core Thesis

Two halves of a man. Book One: Kerouac alone as a fire lookout on Desolation Peak in the North Cascades — 63 days of silence, thought, longing for human contact. Book Two: the abrupt return to the Beat social whirl, and the disillusionment that follows. The thesis isn't stated — it's dramatized: solitude and community both cost you, and neither one is sufficient by itself.
