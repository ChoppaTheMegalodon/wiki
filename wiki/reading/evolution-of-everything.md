# The Evolution of Everything — Matt Ridley

**Format:** Book
**Year:** 2015
**Subtitle:** *How New Ideas Emerge*
**Author:** Matt Ridley (science journalist, author of *The Rational Optimist*, *Genome*)

## Core Thesis

Almost everything important — the economy, language, technology, morality, culture, government — evolves bottom-up through distributed decisions rather than top-down through design. Ridley applies a broadly Hayekian / evolutionary lens across fields, arguing that the "great man" and "grand plan" theories of change are mostly retrospective storytelling over what was actually emergent.

## Key Ideas

- Bottom-up emergence as the default mechanism of change.
- Top-down explanations are narrative conveniences, not causes.
- "Creative destruction" as a universal principle, not just an economic one.
