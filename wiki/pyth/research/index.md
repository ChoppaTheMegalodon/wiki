# Research + Influences

What I've read, watched, and argued with over the years that shaped how I think about community. Not an academic bibliography — a working catalog of the ideas I've actually stolen, borrowed, and repurposed.

If something appears here, it means I've actually engaged with it seriously enough that it shows up somewhere in [[../philosophy|the philosophy]], [[../thesis|the thesis]], or [[../programs|the programs]]. If I'm just name-dropping, it doesn't belong.

---

## How This Section is Organized

Each entry below is its own page. I summarize the core ideas, flag where they show up in my own work, and note where I disagree or would extend the argument. Think of it as a working intellectual journal — not a reading list I want to impress anyone with.
